ABOUT US

At GALAACEE, we believe that the final frontier is not just for astronauts—it's for everyone. Founded by a team of visionary engineers, space enthusiasts, and educational pioneers, we are on a mission to safely and sustainably extend the human experience to the wonders of space.

VISION

To open the gateway to the stars for all of humanity, making space travel as accessible as air travel, and inspiring generations to reach for the cosmos as their next adventure destination.

MISSION

Our mission is to safely and sustainably extend the human experience to the wonders of space, providing unparalleled adventures and comprehensive educational courses in and beyond Earth’s atmosphere, while fostering a global appreciation for our planet and the universe beyond.
